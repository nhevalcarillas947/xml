Operating Hours

Our health center operates on a regular schedule to serve the community. Please note the timings for different services.

  • Monday - Friday 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Saturday 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Sunday Emergency Only
  • Public Holidays Closed

Important Note: Last registration is 30 minutes before closing time. Please bring valid ID and barangay health card for faster service.

Holiday Schedule 2023

The health center will be closed on the following holidays:

  • New Year's Day - January 1
  • Maundy Thursday & Good Friday - April
  • Independence Day - June 12
  • National Heroes Day - August 28
  • Christmas Day - December 25
  • Rizal Day - December 30
