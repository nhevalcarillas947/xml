Comprehensive Health Services

Barangay Paknaan Health Center provides a wide range of medical services to serve our community. All services are offered free of charge to registered residents.

Prenatal & Maternal Care

Comprehensive care for expectant mothers from conception to postpartum with regular monitoring and nutritional support.

  • Regular prenatal check-ups and monitoring
  • Nutritional counseling for pregnant women
  • Tetanus toxoid immunization
  • Postpartum care and family planning
Schedule: Monday & Thursday, 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Immunization Program

Complete vaccination schedule for infants, children, and adults to prevent various diseases.

  • BCG vaccine (against tuberculosis)
  • PENTA vaccine (DPT-HepB-Hib)
  • Measles, Mumps, Rubella (MMR) vaccine
  • COVID-19 vaccines and boosters
Immunization Days: Every Wednesday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM

TB-DOTS Program

Directly Observed Treatment Short-course for tuberculosis with full support and monitoring.

  • Sputum microscopy for TB diagnosis
  • Free anti-TB medications (6-8 months)
  • Daily treatment observation by health workers
  • Nutritional support for TB patients
TB Clinic: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Laboratory Services

Basic diagnostic tests to support clinical assessment and early detection of health issues.

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Urinalysis and fecalysis
  • Blood glucose testing
  • Pregnancy testing
Lab Hours: Monday-Friday, 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Women's Health Services

Comprehensive healthcare services for women of all ages focusing on preventive care.

  • Breast examination and education
  • Cervical cancer screening (Pap smear)
  • Family planning services and counseling
  • Reproductive health education
Women's Clinic: Tuesday & Friday, 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Chronic Disease Management

Monitoring and management of chronic conditions like hypertension and diabetes.

  • Regular blood pressure monitoring
  • Blood sugar testing and diabetes education
  • Medication management and dispensing
  • Lifestyle modification counseling
Chronic Care Clinic: Monday & Wednesday, 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Child Health Services

Comprehensive pediatric care from infancy to adolescence with growth monitoring.

  • Newborn screening and care
  • Growth monitoring and development assessment
  • Nutritional assessment and counseling
  • Deworming program (every 6 months)
Well-Child Clinic: Thursday, 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Dental Health Services

Basic dental care for children and adults including preventive treatments.

  • Oral examination and dental health education
  • Fluoride treatment for children
  • Tooth extraction (simple cases)
  • Oral prophylaxis (cleaning)
Dental Clinic: Monday & Wednesday, 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Mental Health Services

Basic mental health support and counseling services for community members.

  • Mental health screening and assessment
  • Counseling for stress and anxiety
  • Crisis intervention support
  • Community mental health education
By appointment only - Contact health center to schedule

Nutrition Services

Nutritional assessment, counseling, and support programs for all ages.

  • Nutritional status assessment
  • Dietary counseling for health conditions
  • Management of undernutrition
  • Supplemental feeding program
Nutrition Clinic: Friday, 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

About Us

Health centers provide essential healthcare services to promote the well-being of individuals and the community. These services focus on the prevention, diagnosis, and basic treatment of common health conditions. Health centers serve as the first point of contact for patients seeking medical care. Common services offered by a health center include medical consultations, basic check-ups, maternal and child healthcare, immunization services, family planning, and treatment of minor illnesses. Health centers also provide health education and preventive programs to help individuals maintain a healthy lifestyle and prevent disease.
