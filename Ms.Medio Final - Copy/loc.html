How to Get Here

  • By Jeepney: Ride a jeepney with "Paknaan" or "Mandaue-Mactan" route. Get off at Paknaan Elementary School.
  • By Taxi/Grab: Tell the driver "Barangay Paknaan Health Center, near Paknaan Elementary School"
  • By Private Vehicle: Parking is available in front of the health center. Ample space for motorcycles and bicycles.
  • By Public Utility Vehicle (PUV): Available routes from Mandaue City proper and Lapu-Lapu City.

Additional Information

Accessibility: The health center is wheelchair accessible with ramps at the entrance.

Parking: Free parking is available for patients and visitors.

Public Transport: Jeepneys pass by every 10-15 minutes during operating hours.

Walking Distance: From Paknaan Elementary School: 2-minute walk. From Barangay Hall: 3-minute walk.

About Us

Health centers provide essential healthcare services to promote the well-being of individuals and the community. These services focus on the prevention, diagnosis, and basic treatment of common health conditions. Health centers serve as the first point of contact for patients seeking medical care. Common services offered by a health center include medical consultations, basic check-ups, maternal and child healthcare, immunization services, family planning, and treatment of minor illnesses. Health centers also provide health education and preventive programs to help individuals maintain a healthy lifestyle and prevent disease.
